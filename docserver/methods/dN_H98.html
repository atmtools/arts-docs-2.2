Workspace Method dN_H98

Description

Calculation of particle size shape distribution (dN/dR) following
Hess et al. (1998).

A wrapper to internal particle size distribution calculation. The
distribution implemented here is for cloud liquid water,
specifically for continental stratus. The parametrization is over
radius of spherical droplets. Provides number density normalized to
the given liquid water content.

For testing purposes mainly.

Authors: Jana Mendrok
